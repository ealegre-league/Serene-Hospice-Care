Caregiver Training

Empowering Families to Care with Confidence

Overview

What Is Caregiver Training?

We equip family caregivers with the knowledge, skills, and confidence they need to safely and lovingly care for their family member at home.

How It Works

Our nurses and aides provide hands-on education to family caregivers on everything from safe patient handling and medication administration to recognizing signs of decline and knowing when to call the hospice team.

All services are provided at no cost under the Medicare and Medi-Cal hospice benefit. Serene Hospice never denies care based on ability to pay.

🖐️

Safe Patient Handling

Training on positioning, transfers, and mobility assistance to prevent injury for both caregiver and patient.

💊

Medication Management

Guidance on administering medications safely, managing side effects, and knowing when to call the nurse.

👁️

Recognizing Changes

Education on signs of decline and end-of-life changes so families feel prepared and not frightened.

📞

When to Call

Clear guidance on when to contact the hospice team, and confidence that help is always available.

Questions About Caregiver Training?

Our team is here to answer your questions and help determine if hospice is right for your loved one.
